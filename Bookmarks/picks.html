Pickover's Picks

I pulled this list from Clifford Pickover's home page on 17 Oct 1995. What follows is his picks page on that day.
-rr- 
    For some time, I've been assembling a list of interesting web sites
with brief descriptions.  A number of individuals ask me for my list
which is updated regularly with my own entries as well as suggestions
from others.  I thought you might like some of the ones in my
repository called "Pickover's Picks."

    Try taking the interactive windsurfing lesson.

    From frog dissection, to interactive aquaria, to cave art, to Lady
Godiva chocolate samples, all provide dramatic examples of the power of
the nets.

    If you have any others you'd like to add to the list, let me know.

    - Cliff Pickover

  * * * * * * My Tips for the Day:

  * To find the e-mail address of any person on the net, send a note
    to mail-server@rtfm.mit.edu with the line of text:
    send usenet-address/Person's_Last_Name

  * To scavenge the newsgroups for a particular topic, send a note to:
        netnews@db.stanford.edu with a line in the note such as
        subscribe food recipe not fish
    You will each week receive posts related to food recipes but not fish.
    Be specific in your requests or you will receive too much information.
