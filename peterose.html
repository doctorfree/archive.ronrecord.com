Pete Rose Hall of Fame Petition



Pete Rose is the all-time Major League hits leader. He's got more hits than Ty Cobb! We believe Pete Rose has paid his debt to Major League Baseball and should be reinstated so that he can be voted into the Hall of Fame. His lifetime banishment is much more a blemish on Major League Baseball than on the character of Pete Rose, a fierce competitor, run producer and World Series champion. We, the fans, think it's time to reinstate Pete Rose in Major League Baseball so that he can be voted into the Hall of Fame.
