Curdled Turds

 Copyright, Ronald Joe Record
All Rights Reserved
Curdled turds
Maggots wriggling
Burbled words
Drunken giggling

Insults hurled
Abuses tossed
Poison gurgled
Something lost

Curdled turds!
Falling birds
Curdled turds!
Mispelled words
Poop crap shit dump

Racks and racks and racks of crap
The proof in your pudding's not nearly as good
As the scent of the bacon
Sizzling under your hood

Curdled turds!
Falling birds
Curdled turds!
Mispelled words
Poop crap shit dump
