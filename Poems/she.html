She

Written April 22, 2002 Copyright, Ronald Joe Record
by Ronald Joe Record All Rights Reserved 







She


        doesn't love me


She


        will not touch me


Her


        warmth is distant


Her


        fire is dim


I


        cannot leave her


I


        her kisses dream


We


        sit together


We


        in sunlight bathe


Our


        paths are fixed


Our


        hearts are weak


Time


        cleaves our friendship


Time


        repels our touch


Fate


        together briefly


Fate


        betrays our trust


Darkness


        stills the fire


Darkness


        kills the light


Tears


        moisten pillows


Tears


        parch my heart


Silence


        hollow echoes


Silence


        fills the sky


Peace


        descends in mist


Peace


        heralds the end
