No Better

Written April 22, 2002 Copyright, Ronald Joe Record
by Ronald Joe Record All Rights Reserved 







No better lover


        will she find


No better friend


No kind companion 


        confidant


Longingly reaching


        unglorified beauty


Players and partners


        parade across her sky


In stone and jewel


        adorned in light


Her radiance and spirit


        spill freely in the night


I am captive


        spellbound


Agape in awe


        cascading splendor


        hip and thigh


The slightest touch


        the softest kiss


And I am high
