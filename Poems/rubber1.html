Lay Some Rubber On Me

Written October 15, 2001 With suggestions from Tim Ruckle Copyright, Ronald Joe Record
by Ronald Joe Record All Rights Reserved 


Like a squished frog


On a hot highway


Can't get any flatter


Run me over, run me through


High heeled holes in my head


Heart like a collander



Fistulous 

infundibular punctures


Riddled, bewildered and honeycombed



Friable, 

flocculent 
and 

furfuraceous


Perforated, pierced and porous


An 

efflorescent

impalpable



Desiccated

husk


Can't get any flatter


Scrape me off your boot heel


Can't get any holier



Just a stain on a sidewalk


Kiss me quick
