Mesodermic Mary

by Ronald Joe Record

One of my favorite sensations
    The (accidental) whipping of her hair
    Gently sliding across my cheek

What an exquisite extension
    Of her secretly held thoughts
    Escaping in ecstatic wind swept splendor

Let's invent a new dance
    The partners stand a foot apart
    And flail each other with cranial filaments
