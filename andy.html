Andy John Bubble Photographs

Andy took these photographs on Pacific Avenue in Santa Cruz, California. A street entertainer was creating these extremely large bubbles at night. Andy laid down in the street and shot these long exposures capturing the reflections of the downtown lights as the bubbles wriggled and wafted thru the air. Pretty cool.
